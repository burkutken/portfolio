About Me

Mechatronics Engineer

My engineering journey began in 2018 when I first discovered my passion for bridging electronics and mechanics. Starting with Arduino prototyping and Fusion 360 design, I rapidly developed the skills to transform concepts into functional Proof-of-Concept models and Minimum Viable Products. Through persistent self-learning and hands-on experimentation, I cultivated a maker mindset that thrives on turning theoretical knowledge into tangible solutions.

During my 7-year mechatronics engineering journey at university, I deepened my expertise in critical technical domains: mastering FEA with ANSYS for structural optimization, developing control systems in MATLAB/Simulink, and achieving proficiency in both SolidWorks and Fusion 360 for precision CAD design. These years of rigorous study and practical application forged my ability to navigate complex engineering challenges from concept through validation.

Now holding my BEng in Mechatronics Engineering (June 2025), I'm channeling this technical foundation toward entrepreneurial ventures. My vision is to leverage hardware/software integration skills to develop innovative products that solve real-world problems, bridging the gap between engineering excellence and market-ready solutions.
